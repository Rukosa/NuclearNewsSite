Welcome to Nuclear News!

What is Nuclear News?

Nuclear News is a site to give news related to refined searches, in this case "Nuclear" as the title suggests

How does it work?

Nuclear News uses NewsAPI with pre-refined searches to bring the most related news to the pertaining topic.
News is sorted by popularity of the article itself.

Why was it made?

I love reading the news but didn't like those newspaper-like sites which held overwhelmings amounts of info on one page!
